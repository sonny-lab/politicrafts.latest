Chat Board Guide

Welcome to the Chat Board! This is a community space where you can engage in conversations with other users.
How to use:
• Type your message in the input field at the bottom
• Press Enter or click the send button to post
• View other users in the sidebar (desktop only)
• All users appear as "Anonymous" for guest access
Guidelines:

• Keep conversations appropriate
• No spam or excessive messaging
• Report any issues to moderators

• Responsive design for all devices
